## 📺 Slide 2: The Problem
### Digital & Linguistic Divide in Rural India
- **Language Barriers:** 90%+ of agricultural & recycling advice is available only in English, leaving Tamil-speaking farmers behind.
- **Complex Interfaces:** Traditional sustainability tools have complex, desktop-focused layouts that are hard to use on low-end mobile devices.
- **Fragmented Data:** Citizens must visit separate portals for crop disease diagnostic tools, government scheme registration (like PM-KISAN), and recycling advice.

---

## 📺 Slide 3: The Solution
### EcoBot AI — Bilingual Sustainability Platform
- **Fully Localized:** 100% dynamic bilingual interface (Tamil & English) with a single-click toggle.
- **Voice-Enabled:** Interactive chatbot with voice recognition (Speech-to-Text) and audio read-aloud responses (Text-to-Speech).
- **Mobile-First Design:** Optimized, light-weight glassmorphic layout that fits small screens perfectly and stacks dynamically.

---

## 📺 Slide 4: Key Features & AI Modules
### Intelligent Modules for Rural Impact
- **🌾 Smart Farming Advisor:** Personalized regional crop recommendations, seasonal sowing schedules, and organic pest control.
- **♻️ Waste Detection AI:** Upload or snapshot waste images for instant classification and disposal/recycling guidelines.
- **📋 Government Schemes RAG:** AI search and eligibility check for PM-KISAN, Jal Jeevan Mission, PM Surya Ghar, and more.
- **📊 Analytics Dashboard:** Calculates water savings, green scores, and unlocks environmental badges.

---

## 📺 Slide 5: AI Engine & Technology Stack
### Cutting-Edge AI Models & Infrastructure
- **Vision Model:** Google Gemini 1.5 Flash for high-accuracy visual waste identification.
- **LLM Server:** NVIDIA NIM (Llama 3.1 8B) for natural conversational advice and RAG scheme searches.
- **Frontend Framework:** React.js + Framer Motion (premium micro-animations) + Tailwind/Vanilla CSS.
- **Backend Framework:** Node.js / Express proxy server.
- **Database & Auth:** Firebase Firestore for history storage and authentication.
